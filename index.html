Good Evening, Seedling!

A game of growth and adventure, where you choose what kind of tree you'll grow into.

Good evening seedling! After a fulfilling adventure involving falling, flying, waiting and at last sprouting you have broached the earth and are ready to grow into a sturdy and hearty tree. Your environment and choices will impact your particular evolution.

Are you DECIDUOUS or CONIFEROUS? The environment is malleable so if you are UNDECIDED, you can let natural selection decide!

Deciduous trees reproduce through pollination. Some have blooming flowers and some do not. Leafy trees shed their foliage in the cooler months. These trees tend to have a medium hardiness and can grow in most climates and environments that aren't too extreme.

Coniferous trees reproduce through cones. They don't shed their needle-like leaves in the winter months. They are quite strong and hardy, and can grow in a vast range of climates and extremes.

For some trees, it's quite hard to tell what they'll grow into before they reach maturity. Only time will tell...
