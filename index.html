Good Evening, Seedling!

A game of growth and adventure, where you choose what kind of tree you'll grow into.

Good evening seedling! After a fulfilling adventure involving falling, flying, waiting and at last sprouting you have broached the earth and are ready to grow into a tall and hearty tree. Your environment and choices will impact your particular evolution.

Are you deciduous or coniferous?

The environment is malleable so if you are undecided, no sweat. You'll be guided by nature and your own decision making,

This is the first of many choices that will shape your path. Choose how you would like to influence how quickly you grow in height, and how hardy you get over time.

Deciduous trees are known for their large, leafy canopies. Deciduous trees shed their foliage in the cooler months. These trees tend to have a medium hardiness and can grow in most climates and environments that aren't too extreme.

Coniferous trees are known for their needle-like leaves that stay on the tree year round, as well as thier cones. months. They are quite strong and hardy, and can grow in a vast range of climates and extremes.

For some trees, it's quite hard to tell what they'll grow into before they reach maturity. Only time will tell...
