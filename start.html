Good Evening, Seedling!

A game of growth and adventure, where you choose what kind of tree you'll grow into.

Good evening seedling! After a fulfilling adventure involving falling, flying, waiting and at last sprouting you have broached the earth and are ready to grow into a sturdy and hearty tree. Your environment and choices will impact your particular evolution.

Trees generally fall into two major categories. Deciduous or confierous. What would you like to grow into?
